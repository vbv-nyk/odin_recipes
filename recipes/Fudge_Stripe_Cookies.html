Fude Stripe Cookies

Want to satiate your sweeth tooth?

Who doesn't like a chocolate striped cookie? These fudge stripe cookies taste just like your childhood Keebler favorites - but improved with real chocolate and an all-butter cookie. It is made with a simple sugar cookie dough that is very easy to work with and roll out. I used semisweet chocolate to echo the sweeter taste of the Keebler originals, but you can of course use any chocolate you like.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 (12 ounce) bag semisweet chocolate chips
